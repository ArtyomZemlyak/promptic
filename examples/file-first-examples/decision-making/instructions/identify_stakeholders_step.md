# Identify Stakeholders

Determine who is affected by and involved in the decision.

## Stakeholder Categories

1. **Decision makers**: Who has authority to decide?
2. **Influencers**: Who can influence the decision?
3. **Affected parties**: Who will be impacted?
4. **Implementers**: Who will execute the decision?
5. **Advisors**: Who provides expertise or counsel?

## Stakeholder Analysis

For each stakeholder:
- **Interest**: How are they affected?
- **Influence**: How much can they influence?
- **Position**: What is their stance?
- **Needs**: What do they need from this?
- **Communication**: How to engage them?

## Stakeholder Mapping

- **High influence, high interest**: Manage closely
- **High influence, low interest**: Keep satisfied
- **Low influence, high interest**: Keep informed
- **Low influence, low interest**: Monitor

## Stakeholder Engagement

- Identify key stakeholders early
- Understand their perspectives
- Address their concerns
- Keep them informed appropriately
- Involve them in relevant steps
