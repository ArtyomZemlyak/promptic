# Draft Section

Write initial draft of a content section following the plan.

## Drafting Approach

1. **Start with main point**: Lead with key message
2. **Develop ideas**: Expand on main point
3. **Add support**: Include examples and evidence
4. **Connect ideas**: Link to other sections
5. **End strongly**: Conclude with impact

## Drafting Tips

- Write freely without over-editing
- Focus on getting ideas down
- Don't worry about perfect wording initially
- Follow the section plan
- Maintain consistent voice

## Section Structure

- Opening: Hook or transition
- Body: Main content and development
- Support: Examples, data, quotes
- Closing: Transition or conclusion

## Quality Focus

- Clarity of ideas
- Logical flow
- Relevance to topic
- Engagement factor
- Alignment with outline
