# Consult Subject Matter Experts

Seek expert input to fill knowledge gaps and validate assumptions.

## Expert Identification

Identify experts with:
- Relevant domain knowledge
- Experience with similar decisions
- Credible track record
- Availability and willingness
- Objective perspective

## Expert Consultation Process

1. **Prepare questions**: What do you need to know?
2. **Schedule consultations**: Arrange meetings or interviews
3. **Conduct discussions**: Ask focused questions
4. **Document insights**: Record key points
5. **Synthesize input**: Combine expert perspectives

## Consultation Topics

- Technical feasibility
- Market conditions
- Regulatory considerations
- Best practices
- Potential pitfalls
- Alternative approaches

## Expert Input Evaluation

- Consider expert credibility
- Assess alignment with other sources
- Note areas of consensus
- Identify disagreements
- Weigh conflicting opinions

## Expert Input Integration

- Combine with other information
- Use to validate assumptions
- Apply to option evaluation
- Document expert contributions
- Acknowledge limitations
