# Assign Resources and Responsibilities

Allocate people, budget, and other resources to implementation.

## Resource Types

1. **Human resources**: People and skills needed
2. **Financial resources**: Budget and funding
3. **Physical resources**: Equipment, facilities, materials
4. **Information resources**: Data, systems, tools
5. **External resources**: Vendors, partners, consultants

## Resource Assignment

- Match resources to requirements
- Assign clear ownership
- Ensure adequate capacity
- Balance workload
- Plan for contingencies

## Responsibility Assignment

For each action:
- **Owner**: Who is responsible?
- **Contributors**: Who will help?
- **Approvers**: Who must approve?
- **Consultants**: Who provides input?
- **Stakeholders**: Who needs updates?

## Resource Documentation

- List all resource requirements
- Assign specific owners
- Document allocations
- Note constraints or limitations
- Plan for resource management

## Resource Validation

- Verify resources are available
- Check assignments are appropriate
- Ensure capacity is adequate
- Confirm budget is sufficient
- Validate timeline is realistic
