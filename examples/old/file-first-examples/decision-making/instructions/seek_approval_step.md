# Seek Additional Approval

Obtain necessary approvals when confidence is insufficient.

## Approval Triggers

- Low confidence in decision (<0.8)
- High-risk decision
- Significant resource commitment
- Strategic importance
- Regulatory or policy requirements

## Approval Process

1. **Identify approvers**: Who needs to approve?
2. **Prepare materials**: Decision documentation
3. **Present decision**: Share analysis and recommendation
4. **Address concerns**: Respond to questions
5. **Obtain approval**: Get formal sign-off

## Approval Materials

- Executive summary
- Decision rationale
- Evaluation results
- Risk assessment
- Implementation plan
- Recommendation

## Approval Presentation

- Clear problem statement
- Comprehensive analysis
- Strong rationale
- Risk mitigation plans
- Implementation approach
- Expected outcomes

## Approval Follow-up

- Document approval status
- Record any conditions
- Address concerns raised
- Update decision if needed
- Proceed with implementation
