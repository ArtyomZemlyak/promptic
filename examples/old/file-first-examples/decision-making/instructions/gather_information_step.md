# Gather Relevant Information

Collect all information needed to make an informed decision.

## Information Categories

1. **Facts and data**: Objective information
2. **Expert opinions**: Specialized knowledge
3. **Stakeholder input**: Perspectives and concerns
4. **Historical context**: Past decisions and outcomes
5. **External factors**: Market, regulatory, environmental

## Information Gathering Process

- Identify information needs
- Determine best sources
- Collect systematically
- Verify accuracy
- Organize for analysis

## Information Quality

Ensure information is:
- **Accurate**: Correct and verifiable
- **Relevant**: Directly related to decision
- **Complete**: Sufficient for decision
- **Current**: Up-to-date and timely
- **Reliable**: From credible sources

## Information Gaps

- Identify missing information
- Assess impact of gaps
- Determine if decision can proceed
- Plan to fill critical gaps
- Document assumptions if proceeding
