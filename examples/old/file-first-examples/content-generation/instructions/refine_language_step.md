# Refine Language and Tone

Polish language to ensure clarity, engagement, and brand alignment.

## Language Refinement

1. **Clarity**: Ensure every sentence is clear
2. **Conciseness**: Remove unnecessary words
3. **Precision**: Use exact, specific language
4. **Variety**: Vary sentence structure and length
5. **Flow**: Ensure smooth reading experience

## Tone Refinement

- Match brand voice guidelines
- Maintain consistency throughout
- Adjust for audience appropriateness
- Ensure professional yet approachable
- Reflect content purpose (informative, persuasive, etc.)

## Common Refinements

- Replace vague words with specific ones
- Eliminate jargon or explain it
- Strengthen weak verbs
- Remove redundant phrases
- Improve transitions
- Enhance readability

## Brand Alignment

- Use brand-approved terminology
- Follow style guide conventions
- Maintain brand personality
- Ensure messaging consistency
- Reflect brand values
