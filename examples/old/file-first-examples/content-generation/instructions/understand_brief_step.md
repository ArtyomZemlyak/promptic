# Understand Content Brief

Thoroughly analyze the content brief to understand requirements and objectives.

## Brief Analysis Process

1. **Read carefully**: Understand all requirements and constraints
2. **Identify objectives**: What is the content trying to achieve?
3. **Note constraints**: Word count, format, deadline, style requirements
4. **Clarify ambiguities**: Ask questions if anything is unclear
5. **Document understanding**: Summarize key points for reference

## Key Elements to Extract

- Content format and type
- Target audience characteristics
- Key messages to convey
- Tone and style requirements
- Word count or length constraints
- Deadline and timeline
- SEO or performance goals

## Questions to Answer

- What is the primary goal of this content?
- Who is the intended audience?
- What action should readers take?
- What makes this content unique?
- What constraints must be respected?
