# Research Topic

Conduct thorough research to gather accurate, relevant information.

## Research Process

1. **Define scope**: What information is needed?
2. **Identify sources**: Where to find reliable information?
3. **Gather information**: Collect data, facts, and insights
4. **Verify accuracy**: Check facts and cross-reference
5. **Organize findings**: Structure information logically

## Research Sources

- **Primary sources**: Original data, interviews, surveys
- **Secondary sources**: Articles, reports, studies
- **Expert opinions**: Industry experts, thought leaders
- **Case studies**: Real-world examples
- **Statistics**: Relevant data and metrics

## Research Quality Criteria

- Accuracy: Information is correct and verifiable
- Relevance: Directly related to topic
- Currency: Up-to-date and timely
- Authority: From credible sources
- Objectivity: Balanced perspective
